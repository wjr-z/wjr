template<typename Alloc>
struct wjr::is_trivially_allocator< Alloc >

Default construct, destruct allocator.

If Alloc::is_trivially_allocator is not defined or Alloc::is_trivially_allocator is std::false_type, derive from std::false_type.
If is_trivially_allocator_v is true, then construct_at_using_allocator and destroy_at_using_allocator are same as construct_at and destroy_at.
