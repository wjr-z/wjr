inlineconstexpr

Iterator concept that can be used in fast_convert.

The iterator must be contiguous iterator and the value_type must be trivial and sizeof(value_type) == 1. Cast to_address(iter) to uint8_t*(to_chars)/const uint8_t*(from_chars) in fast_convert.
